# EPI Public Specification

Status: public-safe
Updated: 2026-02-24

## What EPI guarantees
- Deterministic evidence states.
- Append-only causal history.
- Verifiable consistency across document identity, witness state, and attestations.
- Separation between decision authority and execution workers.

## What is intentionally not disclosed
- Internal hash composition strategy.
- Internal anti-tamper heuristics.
- Internal optimization logic for incremental progression.
